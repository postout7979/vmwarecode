Overall Message:

Enable our customers to build a private cloud delivering applications and services to the business with agility, policies, management and governance.

Narrative:

Now that the VCF IaaS components are deployed and configured, the organization also wants to provide more of a cloud-like experience using their existing investments and deliver IT services to the business much faster.

The types of catalogue items that businesses will see in the catalogue range from deploying traditional VMs using custom forms to XaaS via scripts that can be run using FaaS methodology within the Automation services. XaaS examples could be things like "Changing an AD user password", "HR onboarding," or other actions requiring an API call to another system.

However, when building out a private self-service cloud-like experience, there are other factors we have to consider, such as cost, performance, capacity planning, and reporting. 

Cost transparency to the requestor and ongoing cost dashboards and reports can help with being used efficiently and alert for future capacity shortfalls.
